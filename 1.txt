Tags:
    There are 2 types of tags.
    1. Paired tags.
        Contains Start tag and end tag.
        eg: 
            <html></html>,<p></p>
    2. Unpaired tags.
        Contains only Start tag.
        There are only three Unpaired tags.
            1. <img>
            2. <br>
            3. <hr>
