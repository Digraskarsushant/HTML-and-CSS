The translate() Method

The Div element is moved 500 pixels to the right and 100 pixels down rom its current position.


The rotate() Method

The Div element is rotated clockwise when we give degrees as positive and vice-versa.


The Div element is rotated clockwise with respect to x-axis when we give degrees as positive and vice-versa.


The Div element is rotated clockwise with respect to y-axis when we give degrees as positive and vice-versa.


The Div element is rotated clockwise with respect to z-axis when we give degrees as positive and vice-versa.
